What is Rotaract?


Rotaract, a division of Rotary International, is a business and service organization for young professionals ages 18 to 30 who want to make the world a better place by doing good. As a Rotaractor you can volunteer locally and internationally, network with like-minded people, build career contacts, develop leadership skills, make new friends and have fun!

Service


From big cities to rural villages, Rotaract is helping communities worldwide.

Leadership


Develop leadership and professional skills, and have fun through service.

Partnership


Rotary club sponsors offer guidance and support while working with Rotaract clubs as partners in service.
